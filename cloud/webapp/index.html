The problem

You've felt it. Every chat starts from zero.

You explain your project, your stack, your preferences — again. The AI nods, helps once, and forgets it all by tomorrow. It's like working with a brilliant colleague who has amnesia.

Aria was built the opposite way. Everything you share becomes part of a memory that's yours alone — consolidated while she rests, the way a person holds on to what matters. The tenth conversation is better than the first. The hundredth feels like she's known you for years.
